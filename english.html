Queen Elizabeth II

We have all heard about Queen Elizabeth II. She is the Queen of the United Kingdom, born in 1926. She was married to Prince Philip and has 4 children. This year she turned 97 years old.

Here are 10 interesting facts about her!

1. She is the longest reigning monarch ever in Britain. She is about to mark 70 years on the throne!

2. Her favourite dogs are corgis. She even created a new breed “dorgi” when her dog corgi mated with a dachshund.

3. She is the only person in the UK who can drive without a licence and doesn’t need a passport.

4. Elizabeth II has two birthdays! Can you imagine? The first one is her actual birthday on the 26th April and the second is in June when the weather is warmer.

5. She became a homeowner at the age of 6, when the people of Wales gifted her a house.

6. The Queen has sent over 50 000 Christmas cards.

7. She has been served by 14 UK Prime Ministers - Winston Churchill, Margaret Thatcher, Boris Johnson…

8. She never went to school. Even though that sounds appealing to every student, she has said multiple times that she regrets missing the school experience.She was still educated, but it was by private tutors.

9. She is not the wealthiest person in Britain. Her net worth is estimated to be 350 million pounds. She is marked 372 on the scale of the 1000 richest people in the UK.

10. She was related to her husband Prince Philip. He was her third cousin.
